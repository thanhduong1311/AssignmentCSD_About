About project

The convex hull of a set of points is the smallest convex polygon that can contain all the points in a given space. The monotone chain algorithm is an effective method for this, based on splitting the shape into an upper and lower hull.
Our project base on Monotone chain algorithm to find a convex by axis of points.

Guide

To get started with application you need to know something

Random a new list of points

To random a list of points, click into the Random button. Application will find a new convex of that list of points.

Move a graph

Hold on blank and change the position of the graph. Scroll mouse to zoom in, zoom out.

Move a point around their position

Hold on a point and change the their position.

Delete a point

To delete a point, right click into it.

Add a point

To add a new point, right click into blank.
